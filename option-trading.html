What is option trading

Options trading is a trading instrument that gives you the right to buy or sell before the expiration time, there are two types of options available one is the call option and the other is the put option.

Call option= It means you predicted that the market would go above your strike price

put option= It means you predicted that the market would go below your strike price

strike price= The price you select or click in the ongoing market for a call or put

Options trading is a short-term trading market that allows traders to book profits in a shorter time period, for example, 1m, 5m, 15m, 30m, 1h, etc. Traders need to predict the market as much as possible before the expiration time.
